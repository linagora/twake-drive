### Human Verification Required

#### 1. Rich Reinjection End-to-End in OnlyOffice

**Test:** Open the Scribe chat panel with a document open in OO. Send a prompt that returns a response with a fragment containing a table (e.g. `[TABLE:0]...[CELL:...]`), a cross-ref (`{{REF:scribe-ref-1:...}}`), and a footnote (`[^scribe-fn-1]`). Click Insert on the resulting fragment card.

**Expected:** The rich content (table structure, cross-ref link, footnote) appears correctly in the OO document — not as raw marker text. The card's visual preview should also show the cosmetically rendered form (table as a real table, cross-ref as its visible text, footnote as a superscript).

**Why human:** The `panelActions.insert(raw)` call is fully wired and the D-03 raw invariant is code-verified by the FragmentCard spec spy. However, the actual OO document rendering (the `markdownToHtml → castPanelAction → PasteHtml` pipeline inside the OO iframe) requires a live cross-origin OO editor — not testable by grep or jsdom.
